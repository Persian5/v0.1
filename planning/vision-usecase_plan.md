## Questions for Vision Document

### Market & Audience
1. What is the estimated size of the diaspora Iranian population? (U.S., Europe, Canada, Australia)
2. What percentage of diaspora Iranians are interested in learning Persian?
3. What is the average willingness to pay for language learning apps?
4. What are the key demographics (age, location, income) of target users?

### Competition
5. Does Duolingo currently offer Persian? If not, why is this an opportunity?
6. What other Persian learning apps/tools exist? What are their limitations?
7. What do users say about existing Persian learning resources? (reviews, forums)

### Product Strategy
8. Why 3 pricing tiers instead of 1 or 2? What's the justification?
9. What features differentiate each tier?
10. What content will be behind paywalls? (Module 1 free, 2-3 premium - what else?)
11. What is the freemium conversion target? (industry average is 2-5%)

### Cultural & Authenticity
12. How does "authentic cultural brand" manifest in the product?
13. What cultural elements are incorporated? (Persian carpet designs, Tehran graphics, etc.)
14. How does iranopedia.com brand recognition help? (cross-promotion, trust, SEO)

### 6-12 Month Goals
15. Is 10,000 monthly users realistic given marketing assets?
16. Is $3-5k MRR realistic? (requires 600-1,000 paying users at $4.99/month)
17. What does "stable v1" mean? (no bugs, all features working, positive reviews?)

### Solo Founder Constraints
18. What tasks can be automated or outsourced?
19. What is the founder's weekly time capacity? (20-30 hours confirmed)
20. What skills does the founder lack? (coding, SQL, email setup, analytics)
